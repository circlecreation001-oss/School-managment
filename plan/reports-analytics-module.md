## Enterprise ERP Business Intelligence Specification

Document Type: Business Intelligence and Solution Design
Version: 1.0
Date: 2026-07-06
Prepared For: Executive, Finance, Academic, HR, Product, and Engineering Teams

---

## 1. Module Overview
The Reports & Analytics Module is a centralized business intelligence layer for the Education ERP platform. It enables institutions to monitor performance, analyze trends, make informed decisions, and generate operational and strategic reports across academic, financial, human resources, and student lifecycle domains.

This module is designed for:
- School and college management teams
- Finance and accounts departments
- Academic heads and principals
- HR and payroll teams
- Parents and administrators
- Executives and decision-makers

The module must support real-time and scheduled reporting, role-based access, interactive dashboards, export functionality, and future AI-driven insights.

## 24. Validation Rules
Validation ensures report integrity and data correctness.

### Common Validation Rules
- Report type must be supported
- Date range must be valid
- Institution and branch scopes must be valid
- Filters must reference existing entities
- Export format must be supported
- User must have permission to view the requested report
- Custom reports must use valid metrics and dimensions

### Data Validation Rules
- Numeric values should be validated before aggregation
- Date fields must be in acceptable formats
- Empty report results should be handled gracefully
- Aggregated metrics should avoid division by zero

---

## 25. Business Rules
Business rules define report logic, access, and governance.

### Reporting Business Rules
- Admins can view all reports within their scope.
- Teachers may view reports relevant to their assigned classes, batches, or subjects.
- Students and parents can view only their own or associated reports.
- Finance reports require role-based access to protect confidential financial data.
- Reports should reflect the active academic session unless otherwise specified.
- Custom reports should be governed by permission and approval rules where necessary.
- Exports should preserve the same filters and scope as the in-app report view.

### Governance Rules
- Sensitive financial and payroll reports should be restricted by role and department.
- Historical reports must be generated from valid and approved source data.
- Reports should include audit metadata for traceability.

## 27. Final Recommendation
The Reports & Analytics Module should be designed as a secure, scalable, and role-aware business intelligence layer for the Education ERP platform. It must support detailed operational reporting, executive dashboards, cross-functional analytics, export capabilities, and future AI-powered insight generation. The module should become the central decision-support system for academic, financial, and administrative leadership.
